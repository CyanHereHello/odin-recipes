Classic Homemade Lasagna

Home

A rich and comforting Italian dish layered with seasoned meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • ½ cup water or red wine
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper

For the Ricotta Mixture:

  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • ¼ tsp salt

For Assembly:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (cooked or no-boil)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a pan over medium heat, brown the ground beef. Drain excess fat.
    • Add onion and cook until soft. Stir in garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    • Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water, sugar, and seasonings. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture:
    • In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, parsley, and salt.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread 1 cup of meat sauce.
    • Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and sauce. Repeat layers, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  4. Bake the Lasagna:
    • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
    • Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
