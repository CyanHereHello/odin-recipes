Homemade Sushi Rolls (Maki)

A fresh and delicious sushi roll filled with rice, seafood, and vegetables, wrapped in nori and served with soy sauce and wasabi.

Ingredients

For the Sushi Rice:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt

For the Fillings:

  • 1 small cucumber, cut into thin strips
  • 1 small avocado, sliced
  • 4 oz sushi-grade salmon or tuna, thinly sliced
  • 4 imitation crab sticks
  • ¼ cup cream cheese (optional)

For Assembly:

  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • Soy sauce (for dipping)
  • Pickled ginger
  • Wasabi
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sushi Rice:
    • Rinse sushi rice in cold water until clear. Drain.
    • Cook rice with water according to package instructions (or simmer for 18 minutes).
    • Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Stir into warm rice and let cool.
  2. Prepare the Fillings:
    • Slice cucumber, avocado, fish, and other fillings into thin strips.
  3. Assemble the Sushi Rolls:
    • Place a bamboo sushi mat on a flat surface.
    • Lay a nori sheet shiny side down.
    • Wet hands and spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch gap at the top.
    • Arrange fillings in a line near the bottom.
    • Roll tightly using the bamboo mat, sealing with a bit of water.
  4. Slice & Serve:
    • Use a sharp knife to cut into 6-8 pieces.
    • Serve with soy sauce, ginger, and wasabi.
