Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

A classic pizzeria-style pepperoni pizza with a crispy homemade crust, flavorful tomato sauce, and melty cheese.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 ½ to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

For the Sauce:

  • 1 (14 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For Toppings:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 20-30 pepperoni slices
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (for brushing crust)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
    • Mix flour and salt in a large bowl. Add yeast mixture and olive oil.
    • Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth. Let rise in a greased bowl for 1-2 hours until doubled.
  2. Make the Sauce:
    • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    • Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
    • Roll out the dough into a 12-inch circle and place on a pizza pan.
    • Spread sauce over the dough, then top with mozzarella, pepperoni, and Parmesan.
    • Sprinkle with oregano and brush crust with olive oil.
  4. Bake the Pizza:
    • Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
    • Let cool for 2 minutes before slicing.
